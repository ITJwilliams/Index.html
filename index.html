My IT Project

Overview

A simplified and less expensive home automation system that is capable of controling lights, heaters, tv, radios etc by touch, sounds or eye and body motion's for people with disabilities and anyboby who desires a simple solution.
Today these things are availible but are very expensive and require specialists trades like electricians and builders to install or electronic whizzes to build. 
Other devices like Google home or Amazon Alexa can be used but wont connect to or operate all devices and settings correctly requiring multiple diffrent home automation systems.
My project will be an elctronic device operated by a native unit or mobile phone and computer application using eye and body motion, sound or touch to control which can operate all these devices and their functions
and using the power of AI to improve the user experience.

Motivation

I've worked with people who have disabilities for over a decade now and i believe there is a long way to go in bringing home automatation to people with a disability in a inexpensive and easy to operate way.
I have seen the diffuclties caused by this to people with disabilities and their families who cannot afford the expensive home systems or dont have the technical knowledge to operate and add devices and functions to current less espensive units.
This would improve the quality of life and bring more indepenence to those with a disability and their families by creating a simplified way for people to operate their own electronic devices.
